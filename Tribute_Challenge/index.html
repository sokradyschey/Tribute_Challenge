Elon Musk

Musk speaking at SpaceX

    Here's a time line of Elon Musk's life:
  • 1971: Born in Transvaal, South Africa
  • 1995: Musk and his brother, Kimbal, started Zip2
  • 1999: Sells Zip2 for 22 Million
  • 1999: Musk co-founded X-Com. An Online Bank (invested his own money)
  • 2000: Merges with Confinity creating PayPal.
  • Jun 2002: Starts SpaceX to build his own rockets with his own money
  • Oct 2002: Sells Paypal for 180 Million after taxes.
  • 2003: Meets JB Straubel Electric Car enthusiats
  • Jan 2004: Meets Tesla founders
  • Apr 2004: Invests in Tesla
  • Mar 2006: First ever rocket launches and explodes
  • Jul 2006: Wins NASA contracts
  • Aug 2006: Becomes first Solar City Investor
  • Mar 2007: Second Launche Fails
  • Mar 2008: First Telsa Customer get their Roadster
  • Aug 2008: Third launch fails destroying 4 satellites aboard including NASA's
  • Aug 2008: SpaceX and Tesla are both running out of money
  • Sep 2008: Fourth Launch Succeeds
  • Dec 2008: Wins Nasa contract for 12 flights for $1.6 Billion
  • Mar 2009: Unveils Tesla first Model S
  • Jun 2010: Tesla goes public at $17 per share
  • Sep 2011: Annouces reusable rockets
  • Feb 2012: Unveils Tesla Model X (SUV)
  • May 2012: Dragon docks with the international space station
  • Jun 2012: Tesla started shipping Tesla Model S
  • Sep 2012: Launches supercharger network
  • Apr 2013: Attempts to soft land rocket from orbit on water
  • Aug 2013: Publishes the design of Hyperloop high-speed rail
  • Apr 2014: Attempts to land rocket from orbit on a ship
  • Jun 2014: Starts building gigafactory to mass produce batteries
  • Apr 2015: Unveils powerwall
  • Jun 2015: Rocket launch fails (first failure after 19 successful launches)
  • Sep 2015: First customer get their Model X
  • Dec 2015: Rocket landing succeed (first reusable rocket to reach orbit)
  • Dec 2016 Launches his largest rocket Falcon Heavy
  • 2017: Gigafactory starts production
  • Mid 2017: First customer get their Model 3
